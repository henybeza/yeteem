Preamble

Yeteem Children and Destitute Mothers Fund (YCDMF) is a high profile Ethiopian Resident Humanitarian Charity founded in 1991under the laws of Ethiopia by a group of individuals tied together by a common vision of a country to mitigate the problems of people particularly vulnerable children and women in difficult circumstance of living. The organization has been re-registered by the Federal Agency for Civil Societies under the new law called Civil Society proclamation No. 1113/2019.

About YETEEM

YCDMF has a General Assembly to oversee and deal with all policy issues. It also has a Board of Directors of five members with the responsibility of the overall guidance and supervision of the functioning of the organization. There is a management committee consisting administration and program staff chaired by Executive Director of the organization to execute programs/projects as well as the day-to-day administrative and managerial matters.

Organizational Principle and Values

Principle: In our human relationship, as an organization as well as individual staff members we uphold the golden rule, “Raising a healthy next generation is every one's responsibility"as an overriding maxim.
